How to Export from MSXI
  1. Go to aka.ms/msxi
  2. Open report: SME&C ACR Service Level Subscription Details SL4 - WW
  3. Scroll to ACR Details by Quarter / Month
  4. Click "Personalize this visual" and add ACR_Parameters Fields above TPAccountName
  5. Filter ServiceCompGrouping to: Analytics, ModernDBs, CoreDBs
  6. Expand all groups in the viewer
  7. Click ... More Options → Export Data → Data with current layout
  8. Save as CSV
